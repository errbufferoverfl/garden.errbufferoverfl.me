# Mediums

gouache

Gouache is a water based medium made of pigment, water and a binding agent, normally gum arabic or dextrin. Gouache is similar to watercolour in that it can be re-hydrated and it infuses with the paper.

woodblock

A technique originally developed in China as a way of printing on textiles and later on paper, the oldest surviving fragments to survive date back to the Han dynasty (before 220).
